Q: I want to propose a special topic, but I’m not an AE, how can I do that?

Q: I’m not sure if my submission should go in a Special Issue or not, what should I do?

A: It is up to you - like the rest of JMLR, we do not accept pre-submission inquiries. Read the submission guidelines for the Special Issue you are interested in, and decide if your submission is appropriate.
